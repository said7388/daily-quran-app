Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Daily Quran - Read & Listen mobile application (hereinafter referred to as the "Application") developed by SST Innovation (hereinafter referred to as the "Service Provider"). The Application is provided as a free service and is offered "AS IS."

2. Information Collection and Usage

When you download and use the Application, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Application usage statistics, including pages visited, duration of visits, and timestamps.
  • Mobile device operating system details.
  • Aggregate, anonymized usage data for application improvement.
  • Location data (only when permission is granted) for calculating accurate prayer times based on the user's location.

The Application does not collect any personal information or share location data with third parties.

3. Information Usage

The Service Provider may use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the functionality of the Application.
  • To send important notices and occasional marketing communications.
  • To analyze user behavior in order to enhance the user experience.
  • To determine accurate prayer times based on user location without storing or sharing the location data.

4. Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Play Services
  • Expo

The Application does not share user location data with any third-party services.

5. Information Disclosure

The Service Provider may disclose user information in the following circumstances:

  • As required by law or legal process.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and property of the Service Provider, users, or others.
  • To trusted service providers who are bound by confidentiality agreements.

6. User Controls

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the Application through the standard device uninstall processes. You may also disable location permissions in your device settings if you do not wish to use location-based prayer times.

7. Data Retention

Any user-provided data will be retained for the duration of application use and for a reasonable period thereafter. To request data deletion, please contact help.dailyquran@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If such information is inadvertently collected, it will be promptly deleted. Parents or guardians who have concerns about their child's data should contact help.dailyquran@gmail.com.

9. Security Measures

The Service Provider is committed to protecting user information through comprehensive physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards.

10. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Application constitutes acceptance of any modifications. Users are advised to review this policy regularly.

11. User Consent

By using the Application, you consent to the information processing practices described in this Privacy Policy.

12. Contact Information

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact the Service Provider at help.dailyquran@gmail.com.
